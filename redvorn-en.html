About us

RELIABLE
INDUSTRIAL PARTNER

REDVORN is a company specialising in industrial welding and pipework, offering high-quality technical solutions to its industrial partners. Our goal is to provide our clients with stable, safe and long-term reliable execution that meets the highest professional standards. In our work, we place particular emphasis on precise planning, quality execution and strict adherence to deadlines. Our experienced professionals and state-of-the-art technology enable us to operate as an efficient and dependable partner even in complex industrial projects. For REDVORN, quality, professional excellence and long-term cooperation represent true value.

Services

WHAT WE OFFER

We carry out complex industrial construction tasks as a subcontractor, based on client drawings and technical documentation.

Industrial Welding

Welding of process and industrial pipeline systems in carbon steel and stainless steel. Our specialisation is pipe welding, where we place particular focus on precise fit-up, quality welds and the creation of long-term reliable joints.

Pipework

Installation, modification and commissioning of process and industrial pipeline systems based on client drawings. In our work, we strive for precise execution, safe operation and the creation of durable industrial systems.

Industrial Assembly

Smaller-scale industrial assembly tasks and installation of mechanical components in industrial environments, as supplementary works within project scopes.
